MACHUPICCHU

The Inca Trail. This is one of the most popular
ways to get to Machu Picchu, hiking over a long
distance in the Andes Mountains, camping on
the trail, and being to see other incan ruins.
The amazing view. Even in pictures, but seeing
the pictures does not compare to actually seeing
it in person.
The Mystery. A lot of people know that it was
built by the incas centuries in what is today
Peru, however, most people don't know that the
site was not "discovered" until 1911.
Other sites en route. Patallacta is one of the
sites you pass by on the inca Trail. This is a
large inca site located near Machu Picchu.
Being an explorer. Cutting your way through
a forest, exploring ancient ruins, and crossing
deadly ancient rope bridges. Enjoy it!
